How to use Chunk 49

Browse Your File to upload

Click on the arrow icon and choose a JSON or CSV(Other file format options are currently unavailable) file from your device to be automatically uploaded and chunked.

Select split procedures for your file

Choose number of lines you want per split for your CSV files and number of objects per split for your JSON files by clicking on the top-down arrow selector. After successful selection, click on “Chunk file” to begin the chunking process.

Success!

Yeah! easy peasy. Now proceed to saving or downloading your chunked files.Undownloaded files are available on our server for 24 hours before they are deleted automatically.

View history

You get to see old and new saved files on your dashboard
